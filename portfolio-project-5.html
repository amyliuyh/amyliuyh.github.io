Planend

Make plan end & Make plan friends

Overview

Nowadays, people lack stamina in carrying out their plans. We found existing countless apps in this field similar and not really useful. So we want to design a new app that can actually motivate users to fulfill their plans.




Competitive Analysis

There're many existing applications about plan and goal. We chose these two apps for they have typical features.
By this analysis, we know the basic functions this sort of mobile app normally have and what they're poor at, in order to find our design opportunity.


We realize that the current apps are similar and have 2 main features.
These apps are focusing too much on the plan itself but forget to motivate the user to persist.
