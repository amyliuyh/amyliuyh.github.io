ECG Lens

Intelligent ECG monitoring app

Problem

In China, there are 280 million patients with heart diseases, death rate caused by heart diseases is the highest. Total hospitalization expenses reach 90.9 billion RMB in 2015.







Solution

We plan to develop a smart app connecting ECG detection devices for non-expert patients with heart diseases, which provides real-time ECG detection, basic intuitive judgement and report based on artificial intelligence.




Market Research

Competitor

Our product will fill the market gap in existing ECG products with its technology and good user experience.

Business Model
1. Artificially intelligent ECG Prognosis API combined with data visualization

Every time a third-party service uses our ECG Prognosis API, we will charge it for one RMB, and we will also offer monthly and yearly service.



2. A smart app for patients with heart diseases




Smart API & Data visualization

Our API based on artificial intelligence can quickly define several heart diseases from the ECG.
