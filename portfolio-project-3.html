Widget Redesign

Nonda Internship

Background

We found it necessary to analyze and refine the purchase interface design based on the fact that the bounce rate is very high at that part.

Also, the add to cart conversion is very low compared to average.




Problems

User Interview:
I invited and interviewed 20 users to find out what they feel about the purchase widget webpage. Then we summarized the problems.

Problems defined:





Target



Design



1st prototype:
I learnt from other famous online purchase websites and designed the 1st prototype. I used the typical tab menu to replace the original list menu.



User Testing:
I invited a few volunteers to check the widget's usability and efficiency. Turns out, people still get confused by different kinds of cable for they look quite alike.

Final design:
Based on user's suggestions, I added small legend to show different kinds of cables more clearly. Then the final design is decided.



Outcome



After the redesign interface is released, big progress can be seen from the data on google analytics. Though still behind average, bounce rate decreased a lot and add to cart conversion rised compared to before.
