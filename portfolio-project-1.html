Contryside area Planning

Tourism village design

Overview

We were assigned to do a countryside area's redesign plan in Shanghai named Shantang Village.

User Research

We interviewed over 200 people from different departments in the Village, including governors and residents, and found three main insights.



As-is Problem

After three-week field study, we defined several as-is problem of this area and redesign strategy.
1.Scattered resources
Rich resources are scattered in the village. Without logical connection, they can hardly realize their value.


2.Lack public facilities
The only place in the village with enough public facilities is the town center.


3.Low landuse efficiency
Almost no industry other than agriculture.


4.Aging population
The young go out for work, leaving the old in the area.


5.Disordered road system
It's hard to conveniently reach every inch of the area. Besides, the landscape is poor and primitive.


Goal & Structure

After research, we decided our design goal --
Take full advantage of extant resources and design a leisure tourism village with agriculture, ancient town, waterside dwelling, etc by designing an 8-milestone cycle route connecting the village.
